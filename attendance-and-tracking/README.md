## Daily Attendance Post

Every class day at **10:00 AM**, post attendance updates in the instructional Slack channel.

Include:

- Students who are absent
- Students who are late
- Students who contacted the team ahead of time
- Any unclear attendance issues

Keep the message short and factual.

## Attendance Policy Reminder

Use the current cohort policy confirmed by the lead instructor.

For this cohort:

- 3 tardies = 1 absence
- 3 absences = attendance limit

## Thursday Morning Recap

Every Thursday morning, post a weekly recap in the instructional Slack channel.

Include:

- Each student's absences
- Each student's tardies
- Each student's homework percentage
- Students who are at risk
- Students who need follow-up

## When to Flag a Student

Tell the lead instructor when a student:

- Reaches 2 absences
- Has repeated tardies
- Drops below 80% homework completion
- Misses multiple assignments
- Becomes unresponsive
- Says they are overwhelmed or thinking about leaving

## Documentation Rule

If it affects a student's progress, document it.

This includes:

- Missing homework
- Attendance issues
- Late arrivals
- Lack of response
- Support offered
- Follow-up messages

Slack messages and the Course Tracker should match.
