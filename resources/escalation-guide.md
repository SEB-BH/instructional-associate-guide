## When to Notify the Lead Instructor

Notify the lead instructor when a student:

- Reaches 2 absences
- Is close to the attendance limit
- Drops below 80% homework completion
- Misses more than one assignment
- Stops responding in Slack
- Says they are overwhelmed or thinking about leaving
- Cannot explain submitted code
- May have submitted work that is not their own
- Has a conflict with another student
- Needs support beyond regular homework feedback

## What to Include

When escalating, include:

- Student name
- What happened
- Date or assignment name
- What has already been documented
- What message has already been sent to the student
- What you need help deciding
