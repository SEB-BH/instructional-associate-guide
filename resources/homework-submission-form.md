## Purpose

The homework submission form gives students one consistent place to submit their work.

It also gives IAs one place to check submissions each morning.

## Suggested Google Form Fields

Use these fields:

1. **Student name**
   - Short answer
   - Required

2. **Student email**
   - Short answer
   - Required

3. **Assignment name**
   - Dropdown or short answer
   - Required

4. **GitHub repository link**
   - Short answer
   - Required

5. **Deployed link**
   - Short answer
   - Required only when the assignment asks for a deployed project

6. **Notes for the instructional team**
   - Paragraph
   - Optional

7. **AI Use Disclosure**
   - Multiple choice
   - Required
   - Options:
     - I did not use AI.
     - I used AI to help me understand, debug, or review my work.
     - I used AI more than I should have and need support understanding my submission.

8. **If you used AI, briefly explain how.**
   - Paragraph
   - Optional unless AI was used

## Form Description

You can use this description:

```text
Submit your homework here by the due date.

Include your GitHub repository link and deployed link when required.

You may use AI as a learning assistant, but you must understand and be able to explain your own work.
```

## IA Check Before Sharing

Before sharing the form with students:

- Submit a test response.
- Confirm links are clickable in the response sheet.
- Confirm the timestamp appears.
- Confirm the lead instructor can access the response sheet.
