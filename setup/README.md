## Before the Cohort Begins

Complete these items before the first Sunday of class.

## 1. Confirm Access

Make sure you can access:

- The Course Tracker
- The instructional Slack channel
- Student Slack accounts or student list
- Homework/lab links
- The homework submission spreadsheet

Tell the lead instructor right away if you are missing access.

## 2. Create the Homework Submission Form

Create one Google Form for students to submit homework.

The form should collect:

- Student name
- Student email
- Assignment name
- GitHub repository link
- Deployed link, if required
- Notes for the instructional team

> 💡 Ask the lead instructor for an example lab link before creating the form. This helps you see how homework is expected to be submitted.

## 3. Test the Form

Before sharing the form:

- Submit one test response.
- Confirm the response appears in the spreadsheet.
- Confirm links are clickable.
- Confirm the timestamp is collected.
- Delete or clearly mark the test response.

## 4. Share the Form

After testing:

- Share the form link with the lead instructor.
- Confirm where students will find the form.
- Confirm where homework due dates will be posted.

## 5. Prepare the Tracker

Before homework begins:

- Confirm the number of required homework assignments.
- Confirm how the homework percentage is calculated.
- Confirm how incomplete assignments should be marked.
- Confirm how tardies and absences are counted.

## Good Enough Means

You are ready for Day 1 when:

- The homework form works.
- The response spreadsheet works.
- The Course Tracker is accessible.
- The instructional team knows where homework submissions will go.
