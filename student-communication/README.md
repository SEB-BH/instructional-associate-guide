## Day 1 Student Conversations

By the end of Day 1, create a Slack conversation for each student.

Each conversation should include:

- The student
- IA #1
- IA #2

This is the official student progress channel.

Use this conversation for:

- Homework feedback
- Missing homework notices
- Attendance follow-up
- Progress updates
- Questions from the student

## Introductory Message

Send a short welcome message after creating the conversation.

Use the template in [Templates](../templates/README.md).

## Communication Tone

Keep messages:

- Clear
- Professional
- Encouraging
- Direct
- Short

Students should understand what happened, why it matters, and what they should do next.

## Privacy

Do not post individual student grades or progress issues in public student channels.

Use:

- The student's private Slack conversation for individual feedback
- The instructional channel for internal team updates
- The Course Tracker for official tracking

## Response Expectations

Try to acknowledge student questions within one business day.

A complete answer is not always needed immediately. It is okay to say:

```text
Thanks for sending this. I am checking with the instructional team and will follow up.
```

## Escalation

Message the lead instructor when:

- A student pushes back on a grade or policy
- A student mentions serious stress or withdrawing
- A student stops responding
- A student has repeated missing work
- You are unsure how to respond
